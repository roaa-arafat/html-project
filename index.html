Butter Chicken

Tender pieces of chicken cooked in a rich sauce of tomatoes, butter, and cream, with Indian spices that give it a warm and distinctive flavor.

ðŸ§º Ingredients

- 500g boneless chicken breast, cut into cubes  
- 2 tablespoons butter  
- 1 onion, finely chopped  
- 2 garlic cloves, minced  
- 1 teaspoon grated ginger  
- 1 cup tomato puree or crushed tomatoes  
- 1/2 cup cooking cream  
- 1 teaspoon garam masala  
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric  
- 1/2 teaspoon paprika  
- Salt and black pepper to taste  
- A pinch of sugar (optional)  
- Fresh coriander for garnish

ðŸ¥„ Steps

1. Melt the butter in a pan and sautÃ© the chopped onion until soft.  
2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and stir until fragrant.  
3. Add the chicken cubes and cook until lightly browned.  
4. Pour in the tomato puree and add all the spices. Let it simmer for about 10 minutes.  
5. Add the cooking cream and a pinch of sugar, then stir until the sauce becomes creamy and smooth.  
6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or naan bread.
